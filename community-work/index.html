Collaborators and Communities

As I have gone careering through my journey on this planet, I have always found fantastic collaborators to who bring both joy and sincere engagement to every project. Listed below are  some (but certainly not all) of communities and collaborators that I have encountered and engaged with over the years.

Sentella and Jozi Burners

Sentella is an art community that throws a little week-long festival in Hartebeespoort every year, while the Jozi Burners are the volunteer corps that keep the Gauteng-based burners connected through regular gatherings, outreach events and training days. I'm an active member of these communities, and I can guarantee that anyone you talk to here will know who I am. I have no idea what their opinions are, but I am sure they will be happy to voice them.
